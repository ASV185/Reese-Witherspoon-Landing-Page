Bio

Reese Witherspoon is an actress well known for her roles in romantic comedies and empowering women, especially for her Legally Blonde movies. She was born in New Orleans, Louisiana, and was brought up into a "southern upbringing". It has been said that her "southern upbringing" and "sense of family and tradition" is who she has shown to the public. Reese Witherspoon is a producer who believes that women should be perceived differently in the media industry, and makes sure as a producer to portray them in a different light than they have before. One of her beliefs is in women equal rights, and owns a media company that supports women in the media industry. Lastly, She is known to be one of the richest women in America.

Her Empowering of Women

Reese Witherspoon founded a company called Hello Sunshine. It "aims to broaden perspective and empower women by giving them authorship, inspiring agency, and creating a platform to help them shape culture and the world around them." Hello Sunshine is a company that wants to change the narrative for women in the media industry by allowing them to storytell.
